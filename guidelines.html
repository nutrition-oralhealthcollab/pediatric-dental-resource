Erosion Prevention (GERD)

science Protective Strategies

Immediate salivary neutralization is critical post-emesis or reflux event.

check_circle Bicarbonate neutralization protocol (AAPD [2])

Hard tissue stabilization through advanced remineralization agents.

Grade 1 Enamel Only

Biofilm Control

For non-oral feeders (G-Tube), specialized chemical control is mandatory to prevent calculus build-up and oral flora imbalance.

Standard of Care

0.12% Chlorhexidine swabs (Little & Falace [4])

Aspiration Risk

Critical risk management during oral care for SHCN patients with impaired cough reflex.

Mandatory Tooling

Suction toothbrushes (AAPD SHCN [11])
