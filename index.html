Understanding the Oral-Systemic Link in Pediatric Feeding.

Oral health knowledge is a powerful complement to pediatric feeding care. Integrating foundational dental concepts into feeding therapy supports more informed clinical reasoning, strengthens diagnostic awareness, and enhances outcomes for complex pediatric populations.
