GERD & Dental Erosion Management

Pathological reflux introduces gastric acid (pH < 2.0) into the oral cavity, leading to irreversible mineral loss. Management requires a synergistic approach between gastroenterology and pediatric dentistry.

check_circle
Chemical Buffering

Use of sodium bicarbonate rinses post-emesis episodes.

check_circle
Protective Barriers

High-viscosity glass ionomer sealants for exposed dentin.

Interprofessional Action

  • Review PPI medication timing (Omeprazole).
  • Assess nocturnal reflux triggers.
  • Schedule dental exams every 3 months for active erosion cases.
