draw Non-Nutritive Sucking Impact

AAPD Policy (2023)

Consistent with AAPD recommendations, persistent habits (beyond age 3) require intervention to prevent:

  • Anterior Open Bite: Significant vertical discrepancy between incisors.
  • V-Shaped Maxilla: Narrowing of the maxillary arch with posterior crossbite.
  • Proclination: Increased overjet of maxillary incisors.

Ref: AAPD Best Practices | Management of Developing Dentition

medical_information 3. Systemic Manifestations

AAPD CLINICAL RECOMMENDATIONS

nutrition Nutritional Deficiencies

  • Angular Cheilitis: Riboflavin/Iron/B12 deficiency indicators.
  • Glossitis: "Bald tongue" associated with anemia (AAPD Handbook).
  • Enamel Defects: Systematic markers of early nutritional insult.

Ref: AAPD Recommendations on Infant Oral Health

coronavirus Systemic Disease Signs

  • Candidiasis: Key indicator for immune dysfunction monitoring.
  • Drug-Induced Gingival Overgrowth: Common with anticonvulsants/CCBs.
  • G-Tube Calculus: Increased risk due to lack of mechanical clearing.

Ref: AAPD Policy on Management of SHCN Patients
