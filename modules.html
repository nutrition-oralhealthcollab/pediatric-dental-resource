Oral Health Integration

hub Clinical Intersections

  • Recognize how oral inflammation and gingival health serve as non-invasive indicators of underlying GI distress in non-verbal pediatric patients.

  • Identify oral soft tissue changes that suggest micronutrient deficiencies, providing early data for nutritional and speech-language interventions.

  • Differentiate between structural dental issues and functional feeding barriers to optimize the timing of multidisciplinary SLP/GI referrals.
