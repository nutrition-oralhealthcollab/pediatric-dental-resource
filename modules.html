AAPD Standard

Non-Nutritive Sucking (Pacifier/Digit)

Understanding the temporal impact of sucking habits on maxillofacial development. Excessive duration beyond critical windows necessitates orthodontic intervention.

Verification Key:

Note: Requires specialized prophylaxis frequency.
